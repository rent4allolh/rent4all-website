Windsor Flat Fee MLS Rental FAQ

What is a "Mere Posting" in Windsor?

A mere posting is a type of MLS® listing where the brokerage is hired to list the property on the MLS® system and Realtor.ca, but the landlord retains the right to manage showings, inquiries, and the leasing process. It is the most cost-effective way to get maximum exposure in the Windsor-Essex market.

Can I list a Windsor basement apartment or condo?

Yes. Our flat fee MLS services cover all residential property types in Windsor, LaSalle, Amherstburg, and Tecumseh, including single-family homes, luxury condos, townhouses, and secondary suites.

How do I handle tenant screening with this package?

Under the flat fee model, you are the primary point of contact. You handle the showings and vet the applicants. However, many landlords use our LandlordEzy tools for reporting and screening support to ensure tenant quality.

What happens if I need help halfway through?

Landlords can upgrade to our Tenant Placement Windsor service at any time. This adds professional leasing agent support for showings, full screening (credit/employment), and lease preparation.

Will my listing show up on mobile apps?

Yes. Your listing will appear on the REALTOR.ca mobile app, Zolo, HouseSigma, and thousands of other real estate syndicate sites that pull data from the local MLS® board.

Do you handle lease agreements?

For the $549 flat fee package, the landlord typically provides their own lease (Ontario Standard Lease). If you upgrade to Tenant Placement, we handle all lease preparation and first/last month rent coordination for you.
