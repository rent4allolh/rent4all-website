Legal Guarantee Coverage Summary

Every Tenant Placement Upgrade and Full Service Leasing package includes a Legal Guarantee covering nonpayment of rent only. Coverage includes N4 and L1 notice preparation, Landlord and Tenant Board representation, eviction coordination, and one complimentary MLS re-listing.

  • 12-month coverage duration
  • Landlord must report missed rent within 5 calendar days
  • Landlord must use LandlordEzy for rent reporting
  • Property damage and maintenance disputes are excluded
