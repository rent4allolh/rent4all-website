Legal Guarantee Coverage Summary

Every Tenant Placement Upgrade and Full Service Leasing package includes a Legal Guarantee covering nonpayment of rent only. Coverage includes N4 and L1 notice preparation, Landlord and Tenant Board representation, eviction coordination, and one complimentary MLS® re-listing if an eviction or move-out occurs during active coverage.

  • 12-month coverage duration
  • Landlord must report missed rent within 5 calendar days
  • Landlord must use LandlordEzy™ for rent reporting and communication
  • Property damage, maintenance disputes, and landlord-caused defaults are excluded
  • LTB filing fees, sheriff fees, and government fees are not included
